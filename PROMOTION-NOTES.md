## 60-second video script

1. Open the Actor and show the one-result `kurti` sample input.
2. Point out `includeAds: false`, `maxResults: 1`, `maxPagesPerQuery: 1`, and Residential India proxy.
3. Show an existing dataset row with title, price, MRP, discount, rating, sizes, image, and product URL.
4. Export the dataset as CSV or Excel.
5. Mention cost controls: start with one query and one product, then scale gradually.
